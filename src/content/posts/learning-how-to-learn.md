This is the moment I went meta. My teaching approach evolved from only teaching languages to also educating students - and me! - on the process of learning.

After reading 'Learning How to Learn' by Barbara Oakley and Terrence Sejnowski, I tumbled down the 'rabbit hole' of the most significant competency in life: learning. My exploration of learning and how to get better at it persists today, amidst the roots and dirt of this ever expanding rabbit hole...
