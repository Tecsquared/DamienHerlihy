Living in the great AI tool flood of 2024, I lean towards specialized tools rather than generalized ones. I prefer tools with an interface over a generalised LLM like *ChatGPT* --- featuring good constraints and clear outcomes. One such tool is ['The TextFX Project'](https://textfx.withgoogle.com) an AI-powered tool for rappers, writers, and wordsmiths.

It's fun to use and leads you down creative rabbit holes you didn't even know existed.
