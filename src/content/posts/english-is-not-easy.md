It can be challenging to discover unique perspectives in the realm of grammar, especially when this space is populated by the Murphys, Pullams, and Huddlesestons. However, Luci Gutierrez's *English is not Easy* stands out as a breath of fresh air compared to the stale offerings above.

The grammar guide features fun - and a little bit adult - illustrations to help students remember difficult grammar rules.

Last year I made a [YouTube video](https://youtu.be/09TH_McbgyU) about the book that you might find interesting.
