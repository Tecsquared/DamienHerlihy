The concept of **Personal Knowledge Management (PKM)** was brought to my attention by the **Building a Second Brain** book, which is reminiscent of **Getting Things Done (GTD)**---acronyms abound in this field!

Apart from the excessive use of acronyms, **BASB** provided me with a structure to manage my life and work. Begin your journey of structuring online information by learning about **The PARA Method** and then advancing to **Building a Second Brain**.

These concepts will transform you from a hoarder of information to a creator of value.
